Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 March 2026

Fleet Checker ("we", "our", or "the app") is a vehicle inspection management application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Fleet Checker, we may collect the following information:

  • Account information: Email address and display name used for login.
  • Fleet data: Vehicle registrations, driver names, and inspection records you create within the app.
  • Photos: Images you capture during vehicle inspections, stored securely in Firebase Storage.
  • Signatures: Digital signatures provided by drivers when completing inspections.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for the purpose of providing the Fleet Checker service:

  • To authenticate your account and manage access.
  • To store and display your fleet, vehicle, and inspection data.
  • To allow fleet owners to view inspection history and compliance status.

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore and Firebase Storage), which provides encryption in transit and at rest. We do not sell, share, or distribute your data to any third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

Fleet Checker uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication: For secure user login.
  • Cloud Firestore: For storing fleet and inspection data.
  • Firebase Storage: For storing inspection photos and signatures.

These services are provided by Google and are subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please contact us using the details below.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and data.

7. Children's Privacy

Fleet Checker is not intended for use by children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us through the Fleet Checker app or via the App Store listing.
