Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a vehicle to my fleet?

Log in as a fleet owner, go to the Vehicles section, and tap "Add Vehicle". Enter the registration number and vehicle details.

How do drivers complete an inspection?

Drivers log in with their credentials, select the assigned vehicle, and work through the inspection checklist. Each item can be marked as Pass, Fail, or N/A. Photos can be attached as evidence.

Can I view past inspections?

Yes. Fleet owners can view the full inspection history for every vehicle in their fleet from the dashboard.

How do I reset my password?

On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password" and enter your email address. A password reset link will be sent to you.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All data is stored securely using Google Firebase with encryption in transit and at rest. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Need More Help?
