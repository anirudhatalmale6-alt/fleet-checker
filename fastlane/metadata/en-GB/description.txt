Fleet Checker helps businesses manage daily vehicle inspections quickly and efficiently.

Drivers can complete van or vehicle safety checks in minutes while managers monitor fleet status and inspection reports directly from their phone or tablet.

Designed for small and medium fleets, Fleet Checker provides a simple way to ensure vehicles are safe, compliant, and ready for the road.

KEY FEATURES

Quick Vehicle Inspections
Drivers complete a simple inspection checklist before starting their shift.

Report Issues Instantly
Drivers can flag issues and upload photos directly from the app.

Fleet Dashboard
Managers can view fleet status, inspection results, and driver reports.

Inspection History
All inspections are stored securely and can be reviewed at any time.

Download Reports
Generate PDF inspection reports for compliance and record keeping.

Simple & Fast
Most inspections take less than 3 minutes to complete.

PERFECT FOR

Fleet Checker is ideal for:
- Delivery companies
- Courier services
- Trade and service fleets
- Construction companies
- Transport businesses

Fleet Checker makes fleet compliance simple, helping businesses keep vehicles safe, inspected, and ready for work.
